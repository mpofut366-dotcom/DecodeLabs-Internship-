## OVERALL FINDINGS 
- The business generated $1.26 million in total revenue across the analysis period. Revneue figures include  all order statuses (cancelled, pending, returned, and delivered were all included in the calculation). The
 reported figure reflects transaction value more than realised revenue.
- Average order Value was approximately $1054 per order.
- Instagram was the strongest refferal source, generating highest customer orders.
- Chairs was the most purchased product, followed by Printers and Laptops
- Revenue fluctuated throughout the analysis period with noticeable peaks and dips across months and years.
- Cancelled orders accounted for the highest transaction value  ($276k), highlighting a large sum of unrealised revenue.
- Payment method usage was relatively balanced across customers, with online payments taking the lead over other methods.
- Coupon code showed no impact on transaction value.
- Product fulfillment outcomes varied across products.
